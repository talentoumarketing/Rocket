Venture Studio led Startup Incubation

We are a Startup Factory, also called a Venture Studio

Rocket is a Venture Studio that incubates and scales Enterprise AI Startups via ideas that are generated internally. We are fully owned by Fund 1 from Spark Capital, a US market focused VC firm with its headquarters in Tampa, Florida.

We build investment ready startups using a Launch-Scale-Exit-Repeat model. Rocket's objective is to get each startup it incubates to initial paying customers and then to at least $1 million ARR.

Our Unique 4 Step Startup Incubation Model

Our incubation model takes a startup from ideation to MVP to revenue and beyond. We do this through a unique 4 step model:

01

Ideate

We incubate our startups from ideas generated internally via our AI Labs. We unearth emerging and unstructured product needs by connecting with the Enterprise Innovation Ecosystem. We then progress these ideas into minimum viable products (MVPs) and initial paying customers.

02

Scale

Startups that get to initial paying customers are nurtured to breakeven and $1 million ARR. Fund 1 from Spark Capital continues to invest in these startups from incubation till they are on track to $1 million ARR and owns 100% of the equity in the startup.

03

Exit

Another 10-20% equity made available for a proven sales leader to take on the CEO role to accelerate the startup to multimillion revenue. The startup exits our Venture Studio.

04

Repeat

The cycle continues as we identify new opportunities and launch the next generation of Enterprise AI startups.

Innovation

Our Unique AI Labs

Our Labs' team leverage Frugal Innovation principles to build POCs (proof of concepts) and MVPs (minimum viable products) in AI. Our core principles on Enterprise Innovation are developed in collaboration with Professor Jaideep Prabhu at the University of Cambridge in UK.

Our Lab teams bring together Design Thinking, Agile Development and Frugal Innovation methodologies. We place a strong emphasis on understanding both internal and external user experience needs.
